Use it as Facial Mask & Facial Scrub

Note: *Always keep it in the refrigerator. Sheet-masks are best to use before make-up whereas Enchant 22 facial mask is best to use after removing make-up due to its cleansing and detoxifying properties.

1. Apply an even layer on face using your hand(in a way that your skin cannot be seen underneath, not a translucent layer).

2. Wait for it to dry (The New Recent formula cuts down drying time).

3. Once it dries, at this stage, if you want to use it as a scrub: take some water and moisten your mask, as it softens, massage your skin 'very gently'.

4. Rinse with water and then you proceed to use your everyday facewash. Use your everyday moisturizer to lock the hydration and nutrition.

Additional Tip: Massage you face with right massaging directions using face oil, so that you can achieve your desirable natural contour with time.

(Since it is composed of 100% ingredients of natural origin, it is always safe and suitable for all skin types, even sensitive skin. but if you have too sensitive skin, remember, you can always do a patch test before applying it.)
